# CapCard Business Product Review - 2026-05-21

Can CapCard be great?

Yes, if it stays disciplined: internal first, evidence-bound, validator-backed, and human-reviewed. It is not ready as a public marketplace.

Potential users:

- Internal AI research teams.
- Safety and evaluation teams.
- Formal methods teams.
- Robotics or simulation review teams.
- Model-curriculum teams.
- Artifact review teams.

Best use cases:

- AI-generated artifact review.
- Capability-marketplace triage.
- No-go claim enforcement.
- Evidence-backed curriculum catalogs.
- Candidate readiness dashboards.
- Review packet generation.

Most plausible revenue paths:

- Internal review service.
- Consulting/evidence audits.
- Hosted/private marketplace.
- CLI/workbench package.
- Command Center integration.

What would make it great:

- A durable source-freshness scanner.
- A static internal marketplace viewer.
- Reviewer identity/date/signoff.
- More real candidate cards.
- Public-copy policy before any public release.

What would kill it:

- Marketing copy pretending local evidence is certification.
- PETAL-style rows described as PETAL verification.
- Hugging Face claims without uploads.
- Stale Command Center references used as direct evidence.
- Public marketplace pressure before the review system is mature.
