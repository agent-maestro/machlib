# MachLib Senses Product Direction

Date: 2026-05-21

## Product Direction

The clarified audio/visual direction is 1op.io/senses: interactive sensory interfaces inspired by animal senses and alternate sensing models.

The goal is not to make report videos the central product. The Evidence Reel work should be carried forward as reusable visual/player infrastructure.

## Useful Infrastructure From The Evidence Reel

- Static card renderer for concise visual states.
- Local HTML player for browser-playable sequences.
- Manifest-driven asset layout.
- Command Center static snapshot pattern.
- Guardrail language for local-only demos.

## Primary Senses Themes

1op Senses should explore interfaces for:

- vision and field-of-view changes
- hearing and frequency ranges
- smell gradients and chemical trails
- touch, pressure, vibration, and texture
- electro-sensory fields
- magnetic orientation
- infrared and heat perception

These should be interactive sensory layers, not static proof/report artifacts.

## Carry-Forward Plan

- Keep the storyboard generator as a structured scene/card manifest tool.
- Keep the SVG card renderer as a reusable visual renderer.
- Keep the HTML player as a local browser preview surface.
- Reuse the Command Center snapshot pattern only after separate review.
- Start a separate Senses prototype around sensory modes rather than package-status reels.
